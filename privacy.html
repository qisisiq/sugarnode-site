Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SugarNode ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application and services ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (used for authentication and account recovery)
  • Account creation date

Stories and Content

  • Local stories: Stories created without an account are stored only on your device. We do not have access to them.
  • Cloud-synced stories: If you have a Pro subscription and enable cloud sync, your stories are stored on our servers (hosted by Supabase) to enable cross-device access.
  • Published stories: If you publish to Explore, the published version becomes publicly viewable.

Subscription and Purchase Information

  • For iOS purchases, Apple handles payment processing. We receive transaction identifiers to verify your subscription status but do not receive your payment details.
  • We store subscription status and expiration dates to manage Pro feature access.

Usage Information

We may collect:

  • App version and platform (iOS, web)
  • General usage patterns to improve the Service
  • Crash reports and error logs

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate your account and manage sessions
  • Process and verify subscriptions
  • Sync your stories across devices (Pro feature)
  • Display published content on Explore
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the Service and fix issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase (https://supabase.com). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row-level security policies restricting data access
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. We recommend keeping local backups of important work.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase for data storage and authentication, and Apple for iOS payment processing.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Published content: Stories you publish to Explore are publicly visible by design.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Export

You can export your stories from within the app at any time.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account from Menu > Delete Account. This permanently removes:

  • Your account and email
  • All cloud-synced stories
  • All published content
  • Subscription entitlement records

Note: Deletion does not cancel active subscriptions. Cancel your subscription separately through the App Store before deleting your account.

Local-Only Use

You can use SugarNode without an account. Local stories never leave your device.

6. Children's Privacy

SugarNode is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:

  • Apple (iOS): Handles in-app purchases and subscription billing. See Apple's privacy policy.
  • Supabase: Provides database, authentication, and storage. See Supabase's privacy policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data while your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: Account data is permanently deleted when you delete your account.
  • Published content: Removed when you unpublish or delete your account.
  • Subscription records: Transaction identifiers may be retained for fraud prevention and legal compliance.

9. International Users

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@sugarnode.app
  • Website: https://sugarnode.app

By using SugarNode, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
